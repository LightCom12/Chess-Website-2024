About Us

Amudan Mangudi

Co-Founder/Asst Head Coach

Project Manager

USCF Candidate Master

As a multiple time state champion and the 2nd board of the ACP-High school chess team, Amudan Mangudi is a chess expert ranked currently within the top 50 chess players in Arizona. He has enthusiastically participated in various chess tournaments for 10 years and boasts a rating of over 2000. Furthermore, he represented Arizona College Prep High School during the 2023 and 2024 K-12 National High School Championships contributing to both a 5th and 9th place team finish. In addition, Amudan is the current president of ACP's Chess Club, with plenty of previous volunteering experiences in teaching chess to kids through different non-profit organizations, He is devoted to serve and inspire the Arizona community as well creating a lasting impact through the Chess Gurus Organization.

Samik was a Co-founder of The Chess Gurus in early 2024. Being one of the top JV players in Arizona, he has a driving passion to share his skills with aspiring chess players.

Kapil Inguva

Co-Founder

CIO/CTO/Coach

As a JV state finalist, Kapil has had a driving passion towards chess for over 4 years. In 2022, he has represented Arizona's JV Chess Team, placing 7th regionally and at States. Furthermore, Kapil has represented Arizona College Prep High School at the 2024 K-12 National High School Championships placing in the top percentiles as a new player. He has also won awards in many local/regional tournaments showcasing his newfound dedication towards chess. He is currently the secretary of ACP High School's chess club and intends to serve the Arizona community through creating a meaningful impact on his peers.

Jayden Lee

Co-Founder

Head Coach

USCF National Master

National Master Jayden Lee is the team captain for the (nationally placing) Arizona College Prep- HS chess team as well as the reigning AIA Champion, multiple-time state champion, and ranked 9th in the state of Arizona for chess. From a young age, Jayden's passion for chess was exhibited through his determination and competitive nature which translated into representing USA at the World Cadet Chess Championship in Spain, representing Arizona at the 2022 Barber Championship of Middle School State Champions, 2023 Denker Championship of High School State Champions, acquiring the National Master title, and representing Arizona College Prep High School in the 2023 and 2024 K-12 National High School Championships held in Washington D.C and Baltimore, Maryland respectively.

The AZ Chess Gurus is a student-led initiative revolving around spreading our passion for chess with young students.
